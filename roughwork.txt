It's similar to other racing games with the key difference being that you drive hovercraft cars that are moving 
at the speed of land-speed record vehicles. There is much less room for error because any contact with obstacles will destroy your
vehicle, with the exception of touching other vehicles. There are a variety of paths to choose on the various courses aswell and
the game includes an open world segment.
